Frequently Asked Questions

What is the primary focus of the club?

Our club focuses on exploring and implementing various AI technologies through projects and informative sessions.

Are there any prerequisites for joining?

Students need to give their interview at the time of recruitment. If they missed the recruitment, their enthusiasm, willingness to learn, and regularity to our sessions will be considered.

What kind of AI projects does the club undertake?

We engage in a diverse range of projects, including machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and more.

How often does the club conduct sessions?

We organise regular sessions, typically weekly, covering topics like AI trends and hands-on practices.

Can members propose and organize their own technical events within the club?

Yes, members can propose and organize their own technical events within the club by discussing with the club members forehand.

How does the club engage with beginners in technology or those with limited experience?

The club fosters engagement with beginners through workshops, mentorship, and tailored learning resources.

Are there opportunities for collaboration projects with other technical clubs?

Yes, we colloborate with other technical clubs for mutual growth and knowledge exchange.

Is there a feedback mechanism for members to suggest improvements or share their experiences with the club?

Yes, we receive feedbacks after every session we believe that feedback mechanism allows members to suggest improvements and share their experiences with the club.

How can members contribute to the clubs online presence, such as blogs or technical publications?

Members can contribute to the club's online presence by creating blogs or technical publications.

How does the club adapt its programmes to cater to members with varying levels of technical proficiency?

The club adapts programs to cater to varying technical proficiency levels through tiered workshops and skill-building sessions.

How does the club encourage diversity in terms of gender, background, and technical interests?

Diversity is encouraged through inclusive policies, outreach programmes, and events catering to a wide range of interests and backgrounds.
